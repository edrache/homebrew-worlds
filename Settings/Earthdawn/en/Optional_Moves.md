# Optional Moves — Earthdawn

---

**Delve into the Kaer**

When you enter a sealed kaer, forgotten citadel, or ruin that has been closed since the Scourge, roll +WIS. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
- You read the wards and markings well enough to know what broke in.
- You find signs of survivors, supplies, or a safe route deeper.
- You notice where the architecture has been twisted by a Horror's presence before it notices you.

---

**Sense the Horror Mark**

When you open yourself to the astral plane to detect Horror taint on a creature, place, or object, roll +WIS. On a 10+, you perceive the nature and depth of the corruption clearly — the GM will tell you what kind of Horror influence this is and how far it has spread. On a 7-9, you sense the taint but choose one: the contact gives the Horror a glimpse of you in return, the vision is incomplete and the GM gives you only a fragment, or the effort leaves you shaken — take disadvantage forward.

---

**Weave a Thread**

When you attempt to weave a magical thread to a thread item, a place of power, or a pattern you have studied, roll +INT. On a 10+, the thread takes hold — you unlock the item's next ability or gain the insight you sought. On a 7-9, the thread connects, but choose one: the weaving draws unwanted attention from the astral, you must sacrifice something personal to anchor the thread, or the connection is fragile and will snap if you take harm before your next rest.

---

**Brave the Poisoned Land**

When the group travels through desolate post-Scourge wilderness — blighted forests, collapsed trade roads, corrupted river passages, or the wastes near the Blood Wood — roll +CON. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
- You avoid the worst of the lingering corruption and tainted ground.
- You find a landmark, old trail marker, or shelter that still holds.
- You spot a Horror construct, cadaver man patrol, or ambush before it closes on you.

---

**Negotiate Under the Empire's Shadow**

When you deal with Theran agents, rival merchant houses, or powerful figures whose loyalty is divided between Barsaive and the Theran Empire, roll +CHA. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
- You learn what they truly want beneath the politics.
- You secure an agreement that does not cost your freedom or honor.
- You leave the encounter without giving them leverage over you or your allies.
