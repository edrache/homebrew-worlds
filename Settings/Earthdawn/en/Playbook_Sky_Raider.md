# SKY RAIDER

*The sky belongs to the bold. You take what the wind offers and leave nothing but thunder behind.*

**Damage Die:** d10
**HP:** 10 + CON

---

## Background

Pick one.

---

**Crystal Raider of the Twilight Peaks**

You were raised among the crystal raiders, where the moot is family, and the sky is law. When you invoke the honor of your moot to demand hospitality, passage, or parley from another aerial group, roll +CHA. On a 10+, they respect the code and oblige. On a 7-9, they demand a gift, a contest, or a favor first.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you survived a raid I led or witnessed?
- Which of you has shared a fire with me on an airship deck under the stars?
- Which of you do I suspect is afraid of heights?
- Which of you carries something I once wanted to take but chose not to?

---

**Kaer-Breaker**

Your moot was among the first to pry open sealed kaers after the Scourge, claiming salvage rights on what the world forgot. When you force open a sealed passage, locked vault, or barricaded door, take advantage.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you came from a kaer my people opened?
- Which of you thinks I treat the dead without enough respect?
- Which of you has seen what can still be living inside a sealed kaer?
- Which of you owes their freedom to my moot's crowbars and courage?

---

**Storm-Sworn Reaver**

You pledged yourself to the wind and storm, and it answers in kind. When you fight on the deck of a moving vessel, on a bridge, on a cliff-face, or anywhere the wind howls, take +1 forward to your first attack.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you has sailed with me through a tempest?
- Which of you thinks my vows to the storm are madness?
- Which of you pulled me back from a fall I should not have survived?
- Which of you do I believe has the heart of a sky raider, whether they know it or not?

---

**Exile of a Fallen Moot**

Your moot was destroyed -- by Horrors, treachery, or Theran chains. You carry its name alone now. The first time each session you take a risk to avenge, honor, or rebuild what your moot lost, mark XP.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you knows what destroyed my moot?
- Which of you has heard me speak the names of the dead when I think no one listens?
- Which of you do I want to claim as moot-kin, though I haven't said so?
- Which of you fears I will drag the group into my private war?

## Starting Moves

**Battle Shout**
When you roar a war cry as you charge into melee, roll +STR. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
- Your enemies flinch, hesitate, or scatter before your charge.
- An ally who hears you takes +1 forward on their next action.
- You close the distance instantly, ignoring obstacles or terrain between you and your foe.

**Sky Weaving**
When you leap between airship decks, swing from rigging, or traverse a precarious height in combat, roll +DEX. On a 10+, you land exactly where you want and may strike immediately. On a 7-9, you get there but choose one: you drop something, you take a blow on the way, or you end up exposed.

**Fireblood**
When you take damage and let rage flood through you instead of flinching, you may deal +1d4 damage on your next attack. If you do, the GM will tell you what reckless thing your fury also causes.

**Down Strike**
When you attack a foe from above -- leaping from a mast, a cliff, a mount, or anything higher than your enemy -- your attack gains `forceful` and `messy`.

**Trophy of the Bold**
When you claim a trophy from a defeated foe in front of witnesses, name the deed. Until you next make camp, you take +1 forward when acting with boldness or intimidation related to that deed.

**Thread of the Moot**
You carry a thread -- a magical bond -- to your moot, your airship, or a treasured weapon passed down through raids. Name it. When you are separated from it, you always know its direction and roughly how far away it is. When you hold it and speak your moot's battle-oath, take +1 forward against a foe that has wronged your people.

## Advances

- **Boarding Master:** When you lead a charge onto an enemy vessel, structure, or fortification, allies who follow you take +1 forward.
- **Blood Oath:** You may shed your own blood to seal a promise. If you keep it, mark XP when the oath is fulfilled. If you break it, take -1 ongoing until you atone.
- **Storm's Fury:** When you fight outdoors in wind, rain, or storm, your attacks gain `forceful`.
- **Horror Stalker:** When you face a Horror or Horror-construct, you may ask the GM one question about its weakness or fear before you act.
- **Unbreakable Moot-Bond:** When an ally within reach would take damage, you may take it instead. If you do, deal +1d4 damage on your next attack.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

---

## Drives

- Claim plunder, glory, or territory that others said was unreachable.
- Defend a member of your moot or chosen family against overwhelming odds.
- Defy a tyrant, a Theran, or anyone who would put chains on the free.
- Leap before you look and survive to boast about it.
