# Optional Moves — The Studio

---

**The Green Light**

When the group aligns on a project—everyone commits, no one hedges—one player rolls +CHA of whoever pitched it hardest. On a 10+, choose 3. On a 7-9, choose 2:
- The CEO approves without reading the full pitch
- The budget lands at the number you needed
- Talent attaches before anyone can talk them out of it
- The room forgets who was skeptical ten minutes ago

---

**The Walk-and-Talk**

When the group moves through the studio lot together and uses the time to make a decision, roll +WIS of whoever is most aware of the situation. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1:
- Everyone agrees before arriving at the destination
- No one with a competing agenda overhears
- The decision is made before you have to sit down and make it official
- Someone says the true thing they've been avoiding

---

**The Oner**

When a crisis must be resolved in real time—no preparation, no delay, no recovery—set a hard deadline. Every player must take an action before time runs out. Each failed roll makes the next person's harder. If the deadline passes, the camera captured everything.

---

**Award Season**

When the group attends a gala, premiere, or industry event where status matters, each player tracks Envy and Status separately. Winning something for the studio gains +1 Status. Not being personally thanked in an acceptance speech gains +1 Envy. At 3 Envy, your next social move is at disadvantage—and everyone in the room can see why.

---

**Talent Management**

When the group must manage a difficult creative figure—fragile ego, impossible demands, actual genius—roll +CHA of whoever takes point. On a 10+, they do the work and it is remarkable. On a 7-9, they do the work, but choose one: they've rewritten something they shouldn't have, they've alienated someone essential, or they've called the CEO directly.
