# THE FIREKEEPER

*Born with a song that opens wounds, heals a room, and draws hungry things out of the dark.*

**Damage Die:** d6
**HP:** 6 + CON

---

Pick one from each row:

**Heritage:** Black Southerner | White Local | Choctaw | Irish Drifter | ask the table
**Age:** young and untested | worn before your time | old-souled and watchful

## Background

Pick one.

---

**Preacher's Child**

You were raised to fear what moves through you. When you openly invoke scripture, prayer, or a hymn against a supernatural threat, take advantage.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you first heard me sing and knew it would cost me something?
- Which of you has seen me break a promise to my family?
- Which of you thinks my gift is holy?
- Which of you has already asked me to play for the dead?

---

**Juke Prodigy**

Your talent was too large for porches and fish fries. When you perform before a crowd that wants release, on a 7+ to **Parley** or **Defy Danger** through music, one nearby ally also takes +1 forward.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you put me in front of a paying crowd for the first time?
- Which of you heard me steal a melody from somewhere unnatural?
- Which of you keeps warning me not to chase applause?
- Which of you saw what happened the last time I played too hard?

---

**Church Runaway**

You learned to hide your hunger behind a clean shirt and a bowed head. When you **Discern Realities** about sin, temptation, or hypocrisy, ask one extra question.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you helped me leave before dawn?
- Which of you still thinks I should go back?
- Which of you knows what I did before I ran?
- Which of you reminds me of the home I abandoned?

---

**Grave Singer**

You have sung over bodies and felt something answer back. When you spend a few moments singing over the dead, ask the GM what in this place refuses to rest.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you brought me to a funeral I cannot forget?
- Which of you has heard me sing to someone already gone?
- Which of you believes the dead listen when I play?
- Which of you fears what might answer me one night?

---

## Starting Moves

**Veil-Piercing Song**
When you sing or play with your full heart in a place of strong feeling, roll +CHA. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
- Draw out a hidden truth, memory, or spirit.
- Give everyone who follows your lead +1 forward.
- Hold back fear, panic, or despair for a few moments.

**Born for the Blues**
When you **Perform** for people in pain, want, or grief, roll +CHA. On a 10+, they give themselves to the moment; ask the GM who in the scene is most changed by your music. On a 7-9, they feel it, but you also attract unwanted attention.

**Hear the Wrong Note**
When something supernatural enters the scene, ask the GM what sounds wrong about it. Take +1 forward acting on the answer.

**Silver Resonance**
When you strike a supernatural creature with a silver instrument part, string, or slide, your attack gains ignores armor.

**Keep the Room**
When you step between panic and a crowd with your voice, roll +CHA. On a 10+, the crowd holds together and follows your lead. On a 7-9, they hold, but choose one: they demand reassurance, they leave someone behind, or you take the brunt of the danger.

**Not Yet My Song**
When death or possession reaches for you through music, roll +WIS. On a 10+, shut it out. On a 7-9, choose one: you shut it out but take disadvantage on your next roll, or you let it in just enough to learn something useful.

---

## Advances

- **Open the Veil:** When you use **Veil-Piercing Song**, on a 12+ choose 3.
- **Borrowed Chorus:** When you sing with an ally, both of you heal 1d6 HP.
- **Hard Gospel:** Your damage die becomes d8.
- **Name the Hunger:** Once per scene, ask the GM what a supernatural foe wants most right now.
- **Last Note Standing:** When you would be taken out while still holding your instrument, roll +CHA. On a 10+, stay standing with 1 HP.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

---

## Drives

- Play a song that tells an ugly truth no one wants spoken.
- Use your gift to protect your people, not just yourself.
- Follow a melody, omen, or story somewhere dangerous.
- Refuse to let fear silence you.
