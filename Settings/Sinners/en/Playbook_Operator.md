# THE OPERATOR

*You can find a room, a bottle, a pianist, a buyer, and a lie that holds together until sunrise.*

**Damage Die:** d8
**HP:** 8 + CON

## Background

Pick one.

---

**Bootleg Runner**

You know how to move heat, liquor, and people through bad country. When you arrange transport or contraband, ask the GM what it will cost and take +1 forward dealing with it.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you rode with me on a run that should have killed us?
- Which of you knows where I keep the good stuff hidden?
- Which of you thinks I always have a second angle?
- Which of you still wants a cut from an old score?

---

**Smooth Talker**

You can sell a dream before the room knows it needs one. When you **Parley** with promises of pleasure, profit, or belonging, take advantage.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you first believed one of my impossible ideas?
- Which of you caught me in a lie and stayed anyway?
- Which of you knows who I charm when I'm scared?
- Which of you keeps me honest more than I like?

---

**Backroom Lover**

You have left trouble in too many beds and train stations. When someone wants more from you than you can give, ask the GM what dangerous opportunity it opens up.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you knows the one person I should not have left behind?
- Which of you saw me choose appetite over caution?
- Which of you came to collect on a promise I made in the dark?
- Which of you thinks I still confuse love with escape?

---

**Club Starter**

You've opened parties in places that should never have worked. When you set up a night's business in a rough place, roll +INT. On a 10+, choose 3. On a 7-9, choose 2.
- Word gets out to the right people.
- Cash starts flowing early.
- You avoid immediate trouble from the law or local power.
- You find one useful person before the night starts.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you did I recruit into one of my schemes?
- Which of you saw me turn a ruin into a payday?
- Which of you believes I can build something real?
- Which of you expects me to run when it collapses?

---

## Starting Moves

**Read the Room**
When you enter a crowded place looking for profit, danger, or weakness, roll +CHA. On a 10+, ask 3. On a 7-9, ask 1.
- Who here wants in badly enough to pay?
- Who here is one wrong word from violence?
- Who here is connected to local power?
- What's the quickest way out if this turns bad?

**Fast Hands**
When you palm, plant, switch, or conceal something small, roll +DEX. On a 10+, no one notices until you want them to. On a 7-9, you do it, but leave a trace, a witness, or a bad suspicion.

**Everybody Knows Somebody**
When you arrive somewhere with money changing hands, name the sort of person you need; the GM will tell you where to find them or why they already know you.

**Ain't No Boys Here**
When someone tries to belittle, dismiss, or box you in, take +1 forward against them.

**Cut a Deal**
When you offer a split, favor, or future opportunity to avoid immediate trouble, roll +CHA. On a 10+, they take it for now. On a 7-9, they want proof first.

**Hidden Blade**
You always have a knife, razor, holdout pistol, or similar small weapon on you unless you are stripped and searched.

---

## Advances

- **House Favorite:** Once per session, declare that you are recognized and welcome somewhere you plausibly could be.
- **Two Steps Ahead:** When your plan goes wrong, ask the GM what you already prepared for this.
- **Velvet Threat:** When you **Parley**, on a 12+ they also reveal something useful.
- **Quick Draw:** The first time you attack from surprise in a scene, deal +1d6 damage.
- **Make It Look Easy:** When you escape by charm, disguise, or confusion, take advantage.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

---

## Drives

- Turn a bad room into your kind of room.
- Win someone over who should know better.
- Build something real out of lies and hustle.
- Take a risk for pleasure, style, or profit.
