# Optional Moves — Sinners

---

**Open the Juke**

When the group turns a rough place into a juke joint, fish fry, rent party, revival, or all-night gathering, say who handled the music, the food, the money, and the door, then roll +CHA from whoever led the whole affair. On a 10+, choose 3. On a 7-9, choose 2.
- The right people hear about it.
- The crowd comes ready to spend, not fight.
- Local trouble stays outside for now.
- Something true and beautiful happens here tonight.

---

**After Sundown Travel**

When you travel Delta roads, riverbanks, fields, or backwoods after sunset, roll +WIS. On a 10+, you avoid the worst of the night and arrive with warning enough to prepare. On a 7-9, you arrive, but choose one: you are followed, you leave something behind, or you see something you wish you had not.

---

**Hold the Threshold**

When something dangerous wants to be invited in and you hold the line at a door, gate, campfire, or window, roll +CON. On a 10+, it cannot cross unless the fiction changes. On a 7-9, it stays out for now, but choose one: it gets in your head, it finds another way around, or someone inside cracks under the pressure.

---

**The Hungry Song**

When music, prayer, memory, or supernatural beauty tempts you to give yourself over, roll +WIS. On a 10+, you keep hold of yourself and may ask the GM what the song wants. On a 7-9, you keep yourself, but choose one: take disadvantage forward, reveal an ugly truth, or step closer before you stop.

---

**Pass Through Hostile Town**

When the group must buy, sell, work, or move through a hostile town where the wrong eyes can ruin everything, roll +INT. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
- You finish quickly and cleanly.
- You do not owe a favor to local power.
- Nobody important learns your names.
- You spot the danger before it closes.
