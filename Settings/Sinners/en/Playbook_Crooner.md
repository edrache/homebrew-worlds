# THE CROONER

*You live on attention, chemistry, and the trembling second before everybody gives in.*

**Damage Die:** d8
**HP:** 8 + CON

## Background

Pick one.

---

**Front-Room Singer**

You know how to hold a packed room together. When you sing to keep a crowd from panic, violence, or despair, take advantage.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you first heard me from the back of the room and came closer?
- Which of you knows what I sound like when I'm truly afraid?
- Which of you has seen me calm a room on the edge of breaking?
- Which of you thinks I only perform for myself?

---

**Married to Trouble**

You have obligations, lovers, and secrets tangled together. When someone tries to shame, trap, or expose you, roll +CHA. On a 10+, they lose the upper hand. On a 7-9, choose one: they hesitate, they reveal what they really want, or you slip away clean.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you knows whose ring I still wear or hide?
- Which of you have I kissed when I should not have?
- Which of you believes I deserve better than this town?
- Which of you saw the danger around me before I did?

---

**After-Hours Star**

Your real work starts when the proper people go home. When you seek rumors, weakness, or appetite in a nightlife scene, ask one extra question after **Read the Room** or **Discern Realities**.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you has followed me after the music ended?
- Which of you knows what people confess to me in the dark?
- Which of you thinks I play a deeper game than I admit?
- Which of you have I made jealous?

---

**Tempted One**

You have looked over the threshold and wanted what looked back. The first time each session you resist an offer of power, belonging, or eternal pleasure, mark XP.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you caught me listening too closely to the wrong voice?
- Which of you knows what kind of forever I once wanted?
- Which of you thinks I might still choose it?
- Which of you keeps pulling me back toward the living?

---

## Starting Moves

**Low-Light Smile**
When you use allure, chemistry, or intimacy to get someone to lower their guard, roll +CHA. On a 10+, hold 2. On a 7-9, hold 1. Spend hold, 1 for 1, to ask a personal question they must answer honestly, get them alone for a moment, or make them hesitate before harming you.

**Velvet Voice**
When you sing, hum, or speak softly over rising fear, choose one ally who takes +1 forward and one foe who falters for a moment.

**Everybody's Watching**
When all eyes are on you, take +1 armor.

**Cut and Turn**
When you break away from someone in close quarters, roll +DEX. On a 10+, you slip free and may take someone with you. On a 7-9, you slip free but leave something behind.

**Taste for Danger**
When you chase desire into obvious trouble, mark XP if you see it through.

**No, You Look at Me**
When you meet the gaze of something seductive, supernatural, or predatory and refuse to look away, roll +CHA. On a 10+, it is unsettled; take +1 forward against it. On a 7-9, you hold its attention, but it learns something true about you.

---

## Advances

- **Steal the Spotlight:** When you enter a scene dramatically, everyone notices you first.
- **Too Close to Let Go:** When someone you've charmed would be harmed, take +1 ongoing to intervene.
- **Killer Chorus:** Your damage die becomes d10 when you attack with surprise, intimacy, or betrayal.
- **Unfinished Business:** Once per session, declare that someone important from your past is here tonight.
- **Heartbreaker:** When someone chooses you over safety, mark XP.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

---

## Drives

- Draw out a confession, desire, or weakness.
- Risk yourself for someone you should leave alone.
- Make a room revolve around you.
- Turn temptation into leverage instead of surrender.
