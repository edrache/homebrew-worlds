# THE HEAVY

*You know how fast a dream can turn to blood. When the door caves in, you are what stands there.*

**Damage Die:** d10
**HP:** 10 + CON

## Background

Pick one.

---

**War Veteran**

You learned to keep moving under gunfire. When you **Defy Danger** under bullets, explosions, or crossfire, take advantage.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you knows what the war made me do?
- Which of you trusts me with a gun but not with peace?
- Which of you saw me freeze once, and never forgot it?
- Which of you has asked me to teach you how to kill?

---

**Older Sibling**

You have spent years cleaning up after somebody you love. When you take damage meant for an ally within reach, reduce it by 1d4.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you have I protected out of habit?
- Which of you keeps reminding me of someone I failed?
- Which of you knows the worst thing I ever did for family?
- Which of you thinks I hold on too tight?

---

**Chicago Muscle**

You enforced debts, threats, and last chances. When you **Parley** with naked violence and show you can carry it through, roll +STR instead of +CHA.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you has seen me collect on a promise?
- Which of you still wonders who I left buried up north?
- Which of you asked me for help I should have refused?
- Which of you believes I can never go clean?

---

**Local Protector**

You stayed when others ran. When you **Defend** a home, business, camp, or community space, hold +1 Readiness.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you did I warn not to come back here?
- Which of you saw me stand my ground when everyone else ran?
- Which of you knows why this place matters to me?
- Which of you makes me believe it could still be worth saving?

---

## Starting Moves

**Gun Hand**
When you attack with a firearm at near range, deal +1 damage.

**Stand in the Door**
When you **Defend** a doorway, stair, bridge, or other choke point, on a 10+ hold 3 instead of 2.

**Cold Resolve**
When fear, grief, or supernatural pressure would make you hesitate, roll +CON. On a 10+, keep moving. On a 7-9, keep moving, but the GM chooses one thing you leave exposed.

**Hard as the Delta**
You count as 1 armor.

**One Good Warning**
When you clearly tell someone what will happen if they cross you and mean every word, roll +CHA. On a 10+, they back off, stall, or show you what they fear most. On a 7-9, they hesitate, but only for a moment.

**Dirty Fight**
When you **Hack and Slash**, on a 10+ you may also choose one: disarm them, drive them where you want, or keep them off an ally.

---

## Advances

- **Tommy Gun:** When you unleash a burst from a prepared automatic weapon, it gains messy and forceful.
- **No Retreat:** When you **Defend**, even on a miss hold 1 Readiness.
- **Blood and Smoke:** When you take damage, take +1 forward against whoever dealt it.
- **War Chest:** You begin each session with one extra useful weapon or explosive the fiction supports.
- **Can't Keep Him Down:** When you would die from violence, roll +CON. On a 10+, survive long enough to finish one more thing.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

---

## Drives

- Protect someone who cannot survive the night alone.
- End a threat cleanly before it reaches your people.
- Pay an old debt in blood, money, or sacrifice.
- Refuse to let a bully decide who gets to live free.
