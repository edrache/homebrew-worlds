# THE ROOTWORKER

*You know the old protections, the old names, and how thin the world gets when grief and music open it up.*

**Damage Die:** d6
**HP:** 6 + CON

## Background

Pick one.

---

**Conjure Keeper**

You were taught the old work by blood. When you prepare a charm, ward, or feeding for an existing talisman, roll +WIS. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
- It lasts the whole night.
- It protects one person or threshold from supernatural intrusion.
- It reveals when it has been tested or broken.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you wears a charm I made?
- Which of you begged me for help too late?
- Which of you has seen my work save a life?
- Which of you fears what I had to pay for my craft?

---

**Card Reader**

People tell you more when they think fate is talking. When you lay cards, bones, shells, or signs for someone and they answer honestly, both of you take +1 forward acting on the reading.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you asked me a question you were afraid to hear answered?
- Which of you ignored my warning?
- Which of you knows I sometimes lie to spare a heart?
- Which of you keeps coming back for another reading?

---

**Healer of the Quarter**

You are the one people bring wounds, fevers, and messes to. When you spend a few minutes tending someone with herbs, touch, or prayer, heal 1d6 HP.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you showed up bleeding at my door?
- Which of you owes me for medicine or silence?
- Which of you stayed with me through a long night?
- Which of you saw me fail to save someone?

---

**Widowed by the Night**

You have already lost something to hunger from beyond the veil. When you act directly against a supernatural creature, take +1 ongoing until the scene ends.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you knows the name of the one I lost?
- Which of you was there the night everything changed?
- Which of you thinks grief sharpened me into a weapon?
- Which of you still sees tenderness in me?

---

## Starting Moves

**Rootwork**
When you prepare a person, place, or object against evil with roots, powders, water, prayer, or blood, tell the table what it looks like and roll +WIS. On a 10+, it works and choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
- It is strong enough to turn aside a supernatural creature for a moment.
- It lasts until dawn.
- It does not demand an immediate sacrifice.

**Feed the Mojo**
When you refresh an ally's charm or mojo bag, they take advantage on their next roll against curse, possession, or supernatural harm.

**I've Seen Worse**
When something impossible happens in front of you, you may stay calm and ask the GM what it wants, what binds it, or what keeps it out.

**Razor From Nowhere**
When someone underestimates you in close quarters, take +1 damage forward.

**House Blessing**
When you sanctify a doorway, room, or campfire before nightfall, the first supernatural thing that tries to cross it must hesitate, reveal itself, or pay a price the GM names.

**Speak Soft to the Dead**
When you whisper to a body or a haunted place, roll +WIS. On a 10+, the GM tells you something useful it remembers. On a 7-9, it tells you, but something else hears as well.

---

## Advances

- **Strong Roots:** When you use **Rootwork**, on a 12+ choose all 3.
- **Bottle the Dawn:** Once per session, produce a prepared sanctified mixture that counts as dangerous and magical against a supernatural foe.
- **Old Name for It:** When you first encounter a monster, curse, or spirit, ask the GM one extra question about how it can be resisted.
- **Bone-Deep Calm:** You are immune to mundane fear.
- **Protective Circle:** When the group shelters where you have prepared defenses, everyone heals your level in HP during **Making Camp**.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

---

## Drives

- Protect a person, home, or memory from the dark.
- Demand a real price from someone who treats your work lightly.
- Learn the true nature of a curse, spirit, or hunger.
- Keep one foot in tenderness even when violence would be easier.
