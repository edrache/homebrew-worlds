# Optional Moves — Star Trek: The Next Generation

---

**Red Alert**

When the ship goes to red alert and everyone rushes to stations, say what you leave unfinished and roll +WIS. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
- You reach the right place before the crisis escalates.
- You immediately spot what matters most.
- You bring someone or something important with you.
- You keep your head clear under pressure.

---

**Lock onto Their Pattern**

When you try to transport someone through interference, combat, weather, or unstable physics, roll +INT. On a 10+, they arrive where intended. On a 7-9, they arrive, but choose one: they are disoriented, something comes with them, or the lock cannot be repeated without a new approach.

---

**First Contact Protocol**

When the group makes first contact with a culture that does not yet trust, understand, or welcome the Federation, say who handles diplomacy, who handles science, and who watches for danger, then roll +CHA from the lead diplomat. On a 10+, choose 3. On a 7-9, choose 2.
- You avoid an immediate cultural offense.
- They reveal what they fear most.
- You learn who truly speaks for them.
- They grant a limited invitation or concession.

---

**Through the Jefferies Tubes**

When you move through maintenance shafts, service ducts, or hidden access ways to bypass danger or reach a vital system, roll +DEX. On a 10+, you get there quickly and unseen. On a 7-9, you get there, but choose one: you are delayed, you emerge somewhere exposed, or you leave a tool or trace behind.

---

**The Directive Presses Back**

When your duty, conscience, and the Prime Directive pull in different directions, name what principle you refuse to abandon and roll +CHA. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
- You find a narrow path that preserves your principle.
- Someone important sees why your choice matters.
- The consequences stay contained for now.
- You do not have to lie to your own crew about it.
