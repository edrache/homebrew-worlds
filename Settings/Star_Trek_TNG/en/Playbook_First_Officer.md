## Background

Pick one.

---

**Decorated Tactical Officer**

You earned your place in combat and boarding actions. When you assess a battlefield, hostile ship, or defensive position, ask the GM where the weak point is.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you saw me win when we should have lost?
- Which of you thinks I solve problems too quickly with force?
- Which of you pulled me back from crossing a line?
- Which of you still trusts me with their life without hesitation?

---

**Executive Mediator**

You keep departments, egos, and impossible schedules from tearing each other apart. When two crew members or factions need the same thing right now, you may declare the compromise you once used in a similar situation.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you I once kept from being transferred off the ship?
- Which of you ignores my authority until it matters?
- Which of you knows how much paperwork follows my miracles?
- Which of you owes me for smoothing over your last disaster?

---

**Recovered Maverick**

You were once nearly done in by recklessness, ambition, or grief. When you choose restraint where the old you would have charged in, mark XP.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you remembers me before I got disciplined?
- Which of you still expects me to go too far?
- Which of you helped me rebuild my career?
- Which of you knows what I am trying not to become again?

---

**Away Team Specialist**

You know how to keep a landing party alive when the plan dissolves. When the group enters a hostile site, you may ask each PC where you stationed them, then name one likely threat you prepared for.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you followed me into the worst extraction of our careers?
- Which of you learned to trust my signals without words?
- Which of you I left behind for one terrible minute too long?
- Which of you thinks I belong on the ground, not the bridge?

## Starting Moves

**Lead the Landing Party**
When you assign immediate roles before an operation, say who watches the flanks, who handles tech, and who takes point, then roll +INT. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 1. Spend hold during the mission, 1 for 1, to have someone be in position, carrying the right gear, or ready for the trouble arriving now.

**By the Book, Until It Fails**
When you enforce procedure in a risky scene, take +1 forward. If the procedure fails because reality is worse than protocol assumed, mark XP.

**Stun Setting**
You know how to end a fight without making it a massacre. When you attack to disable, disarm, or pin down rather than kill, deal your damage and choose one: you force them into cover, knock something free, or open a path for an ally.

**I Need a Damage Report**
When chaos erupts aboard ship, name the system you care about most and roll +WIS. On a 10+, ask 3. On a 7-9, ask 1.
- What's about to fail next?
- Who is already in over their head?
- What can still be saved right now?
- Where is the safest place to regroup?

**Relieve Them**
When someone under your authority freezes, panics, or overreaches, you may step in and take over. If they let you, they clear fear, confusion, or hesitation and you take +1 forward dealing with the fallout.

**Stand Fast**
When you hold a line, doorway, corridor, or transporter pad against superior numbers, roll +CON. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
- You keep them back.
- You protect someone behind you.
- You buy enough time for the plan to change.
- You do not get cut off.

## Advances

- **Veteran of the Frontier:** You always count as having served in the sort of dangerous theater now in play.
- **Ready Room Candor:** When you privately challenge a superior with the truth, take advantage.
- **Perfect Timing:** Once per scene, arrive exactly when another PC needed backup.
- **Boarding Action:** When combat spills into cramped, close-quarters shipboard spaces, deal +1d6 damage the first time you strike.
- **Keep Them Together:** When you rally scattered allies, everyone who follows your lead clears one minor condition or panic.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

## Drives

- Step into danger so the crew does not have to hesitate.
- Turn a failing plan into an extraction.
- Speak an unwelcome truth to command.
- Earn the loyalty of someone who doubted you.
