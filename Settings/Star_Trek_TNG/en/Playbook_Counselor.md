## Background

Pick one.

---

**Ship's Therapist**

You helped crews survive the strain of deep space long before this assignment. When someone finally tells you the truth about their fear, clear one of their immediate hesitations.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you first came to me only because you had no other choice?
- Which of you I know better than is comfortable?
- Which of you thinks I meddle in command decisions?
- Which of you I once kept from breaking apart?

---

**Empathic Diplomat**

You were trained to read the room before translators finish their work. When you enter first contact, mediation, or ritual exchange, ask the GM who here is hiding the strongest feeling.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you I warned about an ambassador you should not trust?
- Which of you saw me end a confrontation without raising my voice?
- Which of you fears what I might sense from you?
- Which of you believes feelings make me less objective?

---

**Listener of Old Sorrows**

You have sat with trauma older than wars and species boundaries. When a scene is haunted by memory, grief, or psychic residue, you may ask what pain still shapes it.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you I found weeping where nobody was meant to see?
- Which of you trusted me with a family wound?
- Which of you thinks I hold too much of other people's pain?
- Which of you stayed with me after I touched something terrible?

---

**Civilian Voice**

You are not impressed by command style for its own sake. When you challenge a military or bureaucratic assumption in favor of ordinary lives, take advantage.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you I reminded that regulations are not the same as morality?
- Which of you thinks I am too soft for this ship?
- Which of you asked me to speak for the people command overlooked?
- Which of you has seen me refuse a direct order on ethical grounds?

## Starting Moves

**What Are You Feeling?**
When you focus on someone in a charged moment, roll +WIS. On a 10+, ask 3. On a 7-9, ask 1.
- What emotion is really driving them?
- What are they trying not to reveal?
- Who or what do they care about most here?
- How could I reach them without force?

**Talk Them Back**
When you sit with someone in panic, rage, grief, or psychic distress and speak them through it, roll +CHA. On a 10+, they steady themselves and choose 2. On a 7-9, they choose 1.
- They tell you something true.
- They do not lash out.
- They can act clearly for the moment.
- They trust you going forward.

**The Tension on the Bridge**
When conflict within the crew threatens the mission, name the person everyone is really reacting to. If they answer honestly, all involved clear the immediate pressure.

**Counseling Record**
When you have spent time with a crew member between scenes, you may ask the GM what pressure is quietly building in them.

**Gentle Truth**
When you say the thing nobody wants said, but say it with care, take +1 forward and whoever hears you knows you meant it sincerely.

**Sense the Fracture**
When you enter a place marked by psychic shock, unresolved grief, or emotional violence, the GM will tell you whose feelings still cling to it most strongly.

## Advances

- **Bridge Presence:** The first time you advise command in a scene, everyone hears you out before acting.
- **Shared Burden:** When someone confesses a painful truth to you, both of you take +1 forward if you act on it together.
- **Protective Empathy:** When a mental, emotional, or telepathic effect targets an ally near you, you may share its burden.
- **Skilled Mediator:** On a 12+ while helping two sides understand each other, both reveal a vulnerable truth.
- **You Need Rest:** Once per session, tell the crew to stand down and recover; if they do, everyone marks XP at camp or downtime as though conditions were safe.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

---

## Drives

- Reach someone others have written off.
- Keep the crew emotionally intact through the mission.
- Speak for the vulnerable in a command decision.
- Learn an intimate truth that changes the situation.
