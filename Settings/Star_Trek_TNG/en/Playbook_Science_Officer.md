## Background

Pick one.

---

**Exobiologist**

You know life adapts faster than doctrine. When you examine a living ecosystem, pathogen, or creature, the GM must tell you one thing it needs to survive.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you I once treated like a specimen before I knew better?
- Which of you helped me save a life I first misread?
- Which of you thinks my curiosity will get us killed?
- Which of you has seen me become emotional over a discovery?

---

**Astrophysicist**

Nebulae, pulsars, gravitic shear, and subspace turbulence speak a language you understand. When you chart a cosmic danger, ask the GM what pattern only you notice.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you still remembers my prediction that saved the ship?
- Which of you ignored my warnings once?
- Which of you I dragged into a dangerous observation post?
- Which of you knows the anomaly I cannot stop thinking about?

---

**Cybernetic Specialist**

You have worked at the border between person and machine. When you interact with artificial intelligence, augmentation, or machine consciousness, take +1 forward.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you asked me whether a machine can have a soul?
- Which of you saw me defend an artificial lifeform nobody else trusted?
- Which of you worries I am trying to improve you?
- Which of you knows what I lost to an experiment in identity?

---

**Archaeologist of the Stars**

You read ruins, relics, and dead empires like living arguments. When you handle an ancient artifact with care, the GM will tell you what danger respectful study avoids.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you first brought me a relic that changed my career?
- Which of you thinks I care too much about the dead?
- Which of you saw me break policy to preserve history?
- Which of you knows the civilization I failed to understand in time?

## Starting Moves

**Scan for Meaning**
When you run a full sensor sweep, tricorder scan, or forensic analysis, roll +INT. On a 10+, ask 3. On a 7-9, ask 1.
- What is the biggest unseen threat here?
- What here does not belong?
- What happened just before we arrived?
- What should we not touch, activate, or disturb?

**State the Hypothesis**
When you propose a scientific explanation before the group acts, say what evidence supports it. If the crew follows your theory and it proves true, everyone involved takes +1 forward; if it proves dangerously incomplete, mark XP.

**Cold Read**
When you observe someone for a few moments in silence, ask the GM whether they are suppressing fear, grief, hostility, or desire.

**Adapt the Sensors**
When ordinary scans fail, you may say what strange recalibration, particle inversion, or experimental method you attempt. Roll +INT. On a 10+, it works without immediate side effects. On a 7-9, it works, but choose one: it takes time, it reveals your presence, or it destabilizes nearby tech.

**Laboratory Calm**
When others panic over a phenomenon you can study, your composure steadies them. Anyone who follows your instructions takes advantage resisting the phenomenon's immediate effects.

**Unexpected Lifeform**
Once per mission, declare that a strange system, anomaly, or structure may actually be alive, aware, or responsive in ways nobody assumed.

## Advances

- **The Pattern Emerges:** When you have enough data, ask the GM what conclusion your peers would still miss.
- **Field Scientist:** You count as having the right sample tools, restraints, and sterile gear.
- **Peer Review:** Once per scene, ask another PC what flaw they notice in your plan; if you adapt to it, take advantage.
- **Brilliant, If Unsettling:** The first time you reveal an uncomfortable truth in a scene, people must react to it before moving on.
- **Multidisciplinary:** Choose another scientific field; when it applies, take +1 ongoing.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

---

## Drives

- Understand a phenomenon before anyone destroys it.
- Preserve knowledge that others would rather bury.
- Treat a strange intelligence as a person first.
- Prove a theory under dangerous conditions.
