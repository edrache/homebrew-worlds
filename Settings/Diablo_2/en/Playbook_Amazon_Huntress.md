## Background

Pick one.

---

**Border Scout**

You know how to read hostile ground. When you scout ahead through wilderness, ruins, or monster-held territory, ask one extra question after **Discern Realities**.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you first proved you could keep up with me on the trail?
- Which of you did I once guide through a place no sane traveler entered?
- Which of you ignores the warnings I leave behind?
- Which of you have I already seen making too much noise?

---

**Sister of the Sightless Eye**

You were trained in discipline, doctrine, and killing demons before they can reach the innocent. When you stand firm against fear or corruption, take advantage.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you doubts my order but trusts my aim?
- Which of you has seen me refuse comfort for duty?
- Which of you carries a secret my sisters would judge harshly?
- Which of you have I sworn to see safely through this darkness?

---

**Javelin Champion**

Your prowess is famous among your people. When you hurl a spear or javelin at medium range, it gains `thrown`, `near`, and `piercing`.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you watched me win a contest of arms?
- Which of you carries one of my broken practice scars?
- Which of you thinks I rely too much on skill and not enough on caution?
- Which of you did I spare when I could have humiliated you?

---

**Monster Trophy-Taker**

You have learned what every creature leaves behind. When you take time to carve, gather, or appraise monster remains, ask the GM what part is useful and valuable here.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you cannot stand the work I do after a kill?
- Which of you wears or carries a trophy I gave you?
- Which of you wants something from a beast I swore to claim first?
- Which of you saw me smile at the wrong kind of hunt?

## Starting Moves

**Rain of Spears**
When you unleash a sudden volley against clustered foes, roll +DEX. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
- You hit exactly what you meant to hit.
- You force them to scatter or dive for cover.
- You keep enough distance to stay clear of immediate reprisal.

**Evasive Footwork**
When you keep moving through danger instead of standing still, take +1 armor.

**Hunter's Mark**
When you study a creature's movement, spoor, or stance, ask the GM one: what is it about to do, what weakness does it show, or where can I drive it?

**Vault and Recover**
When you leap, tumble, or scramble out of a monster's reach, roll +DEX. On a 10+, you land where you want and may take +1 forward. On a 7-9, you escape but leave yourself exposed, separated, or off-balance.

**Cold Discipline**
When you refuse panic, temptation, or a demonic whisper, roll +WIS. On a 10+, hold steady and mark XP. On a 7-9, you hold steady, but the GM will tell you what it cost.

**Spear in the Dark**
When you strike first from ambush, concealment, or superior position, your attack gains `piercing`.

## Advances

- **Pierce the Screen:** Your ranged attacks ignore the first point of armor from shields, chitin, or cover.
- **No Escape:** When a wounded enemy tries to flee, you always know which way it ran.
- **Battle Reflexes:** The first time each fight you would be surrounded, you may immediately reposition somewhere nearby.
- **Queen of the Hunt:** When you bring back proof of a kill, take +1 forward with people who fear that prey.
- **Storm of Iron:** When you deal damage with a thrown weapon, you may hit another nearby foe for 1 damage.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

---

## Drives

- Bring down a monster that others fear to face.
- Reach a vantage point no one else can hold.
- Prove your discipline under pressure.
- Take a worthy trophy from a dangerous kill.
