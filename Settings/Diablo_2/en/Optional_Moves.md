# Optional Moves — Diablo 2

---

**Through Corrupted Wilderness**

When the group travels through blighted forests, cursed deserts, frozen passes, or demon-haunted roads, roll +WIS. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
- You avoid the worst ambushes and dead ground.
- You find signs of safety, shelter, or old shrines.
- You spot corruption before it reaches for you.

---

**Search the Ruins**

When you take time to search a crypt, temple, battlefield, sewer, or shattered stronghold for something valuable or revealing, roll +INT. On a 10+, ask 2. On a 7-9, ask 1.
- What was hidden here on purpose?
- What here is still dangerous?
- What here points to the greater evil behind this place?

---

**Resist Corruption**

When a demonic relic, profane ritual, cursed place, or whispering evil offers power, safety, or temptation, roll +WIS. On a 10+, you refuse it and see one of its true intentions. On a 7-9, you refuse it, but choose one: take disadvantage forward, reveal a weakness, or carry a lingering mark of that contact.

---

**Town Under Siege**

When you try to organize frightened townsfolk while monsters press at the walls, roll +CHA. On a 10+, choose 3. On a 7-9, choose 2.
- The people hold together.
- You uncover a useful defender, route, or stockpile.
- Panic does not become betrayal.
- The town remembers who stood for it.

---

**Pursue the Prime Evil**

When you chase a powerful servant of darkness across regions, ask each player how they help keep the pursuit alive, then roll +CON from whoever bears the burden most directly. On a 10+, you close the distance. On a 7-9, you keep the trail, but choose one: you lose time, you lose supplies, or something else reaches the quarry first.
