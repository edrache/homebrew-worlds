## Background

Pick one.

---

**Vizjerei Student**

You were taught theory before survival. When you recall lore about arcane rituals, demonic pacts, or forbidden libraries, ask the GM what dangerous truth you know.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you has already seen one of my spells go wrong?
- Which of you thinks my order caused more ruin than wisdom?
- Which of you did I question as if you were a specimen?
- Which of you carries a mystery I cannot stop studying?

---

**Battle Arcanist**

You learned to cast under pressure. When you unleash elemental power while under attack, you do not automatically expose yourself to immediate reprisal.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you saw me end a fight in one terrible burst?
- Which of you knows how exhausted I look after real magic?
- Which of you wants my power aimed at a personal enemy?
- Which of you do I trust to drag me clear if the spell takes too much?

---

**Keeper of Lost Formulae**

You possess pages no one else should have. When you study, bargain over, or decode arcane writing, take advantage.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you has read over my shoulder when you should not have?
- Which of you helped me steal or save these pages?
- Which of you fears what might happen if I finish that research?
- Which of you asked me to find a spell I should refuse?

---

**Storm-Touched**

Magic answered you before any teacher did. The first time each session you choose reckless power over caution, mark XP.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you saw the sign that marked me as dangerous?
- Which of you thinks I am only barely in control?
- Which of you has felt static, heat, or frost crawl over your skin when I am angry?
- Which of you still treats me like a person instead of a hazard?

## Starting Moves

**Elemental Bolt**
Choose fire, lightning, or frost. When you hurl that element as a weapon, roll +INT. On a hit, deal your damage and choose one: `near`, `forceful`, or the target is momentarily hindered by flame, shock, or rime.

**Teleport Step**
When you blink a short distance to a place you can clearly see, roll +INT. On a 10+, arrive exactly where you want. On a 7-9, arrive but choose one: leave something behind, draw attention, or suffer a magical backlash.

**Arcane Ward**
When you shape raw power into protection, gain +1 armor until you cast destructively, move recklessly, or let the ward drop.

**Frozen Moment**
When you encase a person, weapon, door, or small area in bitter frost, tell the GM what you are trying to halt and roll +INT. On a 10+, it is held fast for now. On a 7-9, it is slowed or brittle, but not fully stopped.

**Read the Ley**
When you pause to sense the nearest source of magic, corruption, or planar strain, ask the GM where it is strongest and what it wants.

**Glass Cannon**
When you accept disadvantage on a spell to make it terrifyingly obvious, add `messy` or `forceful` to its effects.

## Advances

- **Chain Lightning:** When your lightning strikes true, you may arc 1 damage to another nearby target.
- **Fire Mastery:** Your flames can illuminate, cauterize, or consume mundane barriers without a roll when the fiction allows.
- **Ice Prison:** When you freeze a foe in place, its armor counts as 1 lower until it breaks free.
- **Arcane Recall:** Once per session, produce a minor but useful magical effect you prepared earlier.
- **Mana Hunger:** When you draw from a dangerous source of power, ask the GM what corruption comes with it and take +1 forward anyway.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

---

## Drives

- Solve a problem with forbidden or spectacular magic.
- Learn the true nature of a supernatural threat.
- Push your power beyond safe limits.
- Keep magical knowledge out of the wrong hands.
