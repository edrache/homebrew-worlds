# THE CLERIC

*You carry the weight of the Church on your back and the word of the One True God on your lips. You have seen what walks the dark of Dolmenwood. You know what it costs to push it back.*

**Damage Die:** d6
**HP:** 6 + CON

## Background

Pick one.

---

**Order of St Signis — Lichward**

You are trained to hunt the walking dead and protect the graves of the faithful. When you attack undead, take +1 forward. Your attacks harm undead that can only be harmed by magical or silver weapons, even without such weapons.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you has watched me destroy something that couldn't be killed by ordinary means?
- Which of you is unsettled by how calm I am around the dead?
- Which of you has asked me to say words over someone we lost?
- Which of you worries I'm too focused on death to notice the living?

---

**Order of St Sedge — Defender**

You are trained to protect Church lands and the innocent within them. When you Defend a person, settlement, or sacred place, hold +1 Readiness. When you lay hands on a wounded character, you may heal up to 1 HP per Level, once per day.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you have I stood between and something dangerous?
- Which of you doesn't think they need protecting?
- Which of you has seen me take a wound I should have avoided?
- Which of you reminds me of someone I failed to protect?

---

**Seminary-Trained**

You studied at the Seminary of 100 Martyrs. You know the Church's doctrine, its politics, and its secrets. When you Spout Lore about the Church, its saints, or religious history, take +1 forward.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you knows my reputation from my seminary days?
- Which of you has asked me something the Church would prefer I not answer?
- Which of you thinks I'm too bound by doctrine?
- Which of you has seen me act against my orders for the right reasons?

---

**Wandering Convert**

You came to the faith late, through experience rather than doctrine. Your belief is harder-won and fiercer. When you act on your faith in a moment of genuine danger — without armour, without backup, without certainty — take +1 forward.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you knows what I was before the Church found me?
- Which of you has challenged my faith and been surprised by what I said?
- Which of you I would have robbed or wronged in my old life?
- Which of you believes in something different, and hasn't told me yet?

## Starting Moves

**Turn the Undead**
When you present your holy symbol and invoke the One True God against the undead, roll +WIS. On a 10+, 2d4 undead flee or are stunned. On a 7-9, 1d4 undead are affected but choose one: they're only delayed, they turn on you when the effect ends, or you lose hold of your symbol.

**Holy Magic**
Beginning at Level 2, you may pray for spells each day. You know prayers appropriate to your Order and your experience. Losing faith or acting against Church tenets removes this ability until you atone.

**Armour of Conviction**
When you have visibly prepared — armour on, symbol ready, allies gathered — take +1 armour (in addition to your worn armour).

**Liturgic**
You speak Liturgic, the scriptural tongue. When you invoke Church authority in writing, ritual, or formal address, those who recognise it must take you seriously.

**Purge the Dark**
When you act directly against the undead, the Drune, or malign fairy magic, your attacks count as magical. You take no penalty from weapons or armour that would normally restrict the faithful.

**Tenets of the Church**
You are sworn to Evangelism, Hierarchy, Monotheism, and the Sanctity of Life. When you act decisively in line with these tenets in a difficult moment — at real cost to yourself — mark XP.

## Advances

- **Writ of Determination:** The Church grants you formal freedom to act independently. When you invoke this authority, even superiors must pause and listen.
- **Chain-Break:** When you Defy Danger against supernatural fear, take +1 forward. You have faced worse.
- **Holy Ground:** When you consecrate a space with prayer and ritual (takes one turn), undead cannot enter it and chaotic magic is weakened within it.
- **Exorcism:** When you lay hands on a possessed or supernaturally afflicted creature and pray for its release, roll +WIS. On a 10+, the affliction is expelled. On a 7-9, it's weakened and you choose one: it harms you, it takes root nearby, or the creature is left dangerously unstable.
- **Rally the Flock:** When you call out to allies in battle, they clear Fear and take +1 forward to their next action.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

---

## Drives

- Drive back something dark that no one else could face.
- Bring one lost soul into the faith — not by force, but by example.
- Uphold a Church tenet when it costs you something real.
- Find and put to rest something that shouldn't still be walking.
