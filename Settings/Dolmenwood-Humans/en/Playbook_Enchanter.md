# THE ENCHANTER

*You were touched by Fairy and didn't come back the same. The glamours live in your hands now. You're still human — mostly — but you dream in Sylvan and sometimes the trees answer back.*

**Damage Die:** d6
**HP:** 6 + CON

## Background

Pick one.

---

**Stolen Child, Returned**

You were taken by a fairy noble as a child and released — or escaped — years later. You know the courtesy of their courts and the price of their gifts. When you treat with a fairy creature using proper etiquette, take +1 forward.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you noticed that I sometimes forget what year it is?
- Which of you has asked me what happened in Fairy — and gotten the real answer?
- Which of you is wary of me because of what I might still owe?
- Which of you treats me as fully human, no questions asked?

---

**Rune Scholar**

You sought out fairy magic deliberately, trading service or secrets to earn your first runes. When you study a fairy rune, location, or enchantment, you may ask the GM one question about its nature or purpose. The GM answers honestly.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you is uncomfortable with how deliberately I pursued this?
- Which of you has asked to see my runes and I showed them?
- Which of you knows what I traded for my first glamour?
- Which of you I would trust with a fairy secret I haven't told anyone else?

---

**Mixed Blood, Distant**

Your fairy ancestry is far enough removed that no one sees it — except in what you can do. You pass entirely as ordinary. When you conceal your abilities and act as an unremarkable traveler, take +1 forward to Defy Danger against social detection.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you figured out what I am before I told anyone?
- Which of you has helped me keep it quiet?
- Which of you sees me as more fairy than I am?
- Which of you I trust most with the truth?

---

**Marked Without Warning**

You woke one morning with a rune carved faintly into your skin. No explanation, no memory, no context — just power and a debt you can't name. When you act on instinct with a glamour, without stopping to think, take +1 forward.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you was with me when I first used it?
- Which of you is worried the debt will come due?
- Which of you has tried to read the mark and told me what they found?
- Which of you I fear will be affected when I finally owe up?

## Starting Moves

**Glamours**
You possess minor magical talents called glamours. At Level 1, you know one glamour selected randomly with the GM. Glamours are tricks of fairy magic — illusions, beguilements, vanishings, small transformations. When you use a glamour, roll +CHA. On a 10+, it works as intended. On a 7-9, it works but choose one: it's visible to those who know what to look for, it costs something small, or it affects more than intended.

**Fairy Rune**
You know one rune of lesser magnitude, granted by a fairy noble (or marked on you unbidden). When you invoke the rune, roll +INT. The GM determines the magnitude of effect. Acquiring new runes requires winning the favour of fairy nobility or surviving events that bring them to you.

**Detect Magic**
When you concentrate on a person, object, or location, you sense the presence and approximate nature of fairy or arcane magic. No roll required.

**Sylvan Tongue**
You speak Sylvan, the language of Fairy. You may address fairy creatures, read fairy inscriptions, and navigate their courts without the ignorance that costs mortals so dearly.

**Otherworldly Guise**
When you draw on your fairy connection to appear more fey — slightly luminous, eerily still, otherworldly — roll +CHA. On a 10+, mortals treat you with cautious deference. Fairies assess you as a peer. On a 7-9, choose one: mortals are suspicious, or fairies demand proof of your lineage.

**Beguile**
When you catch a creature's eye and hold it, roll +CHA. On a 10+, they are beguiled — they can't act against you for one scene and believe, vaguely, that you mean them well. On a 7-9, they are distracted and uncertain.

## Advances

- **New Glamour:** Learn an additional glamour, rolled randomly with the GM.
- **Greater Rune:** A fairy noble grants you a rune of greater magnitude, for a favour named or pending.
- **Fairy Road:** You know a fairy road in Dolmenwood. Once per session, you may use it to travel quickly or enter a fairy realm, though the GM sets the terms.
- **Shimmering Ward:** When you weave a glamour around a space or person, mundane intruders cannot perceive it unless they actively search and roll to do so.
- **Silver Tongue of Fairy:** When you negotiate with a fairy creature using the proper forms of courtesy, the GM must offer you at least one real option rather than dismissal.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

## Drives

- Learn the name of the fairy noble who marked or changed you.
- Gain a rune no mortal has held before.
- Help another mortal survive an encounter with Fairy that would have destroyed them.
- Discover whether you can ever fully return to what you were.
