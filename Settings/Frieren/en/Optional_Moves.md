# Optional Moves — Frieren: Beyond Journey's End

## Optional Moves

**Reading the Mana**
When you carefully observe someone cast magic, suppress their power, or fight using a magical technique, roll +INT. On a 10+, ask the GM two questions from the list below. On a 7-9, ask one:
- How powerful are they really?
- What are they trying to hide?
- What type of magic are they preparing?
- What would they not want me to know about their limits?

---

**Against the Clock**
When you push through failing health, exhaustion, or rapidly closing time to complete something vital, roll +CON. On a 10+, you finish in time and mark XP. On a 7-9, choose one: you finish but take a debility, or you don't quite finish but hold on long enough for someone else to.

---

**Pulling Someone Back**
When you confront a person trapped in stagnation, resignation, or fear and offer them a concrete reason to move forward, roll +CHA. On a 10+, something in them shifts — they act on it and mark XP. On a 7-9, they hear you, but something still holds them. Tell the GM what it is.

---

**Ancient Ground**
When you travel through a place touched by old magic, long-dead heroes, or past catastrophe, roll +WIS. On a 10+, the GM gives you two true pieces of information about what happened here. On a 7-9, you learn one true thing — but something here takes notice of you.

---

**Leave Something Behind**
When you ensure that a person, deed, or memory will outlive you — through a student, a monument, a written record, or a final act carried out at cost — mark XP. If what you leave is connected to someone already gone, mark XP again.
