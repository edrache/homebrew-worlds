# THE PRODIGY

*You don't need ancient wisdom or innate genius. You need discipline, time, and the willingness to work harder than everyone expects you to. Your magic isn't spectacular. It doesn't need to be — it just needs to work.*

**HP:** 6 + CON

## Background

Pick one.

---

**Orphan's Debt**

Your guardian gave their remaining years for you. That matters more than they ever let on. When you honor their legacy through action — completing what they started, protecting who they loved — mark XP.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you knows what was sacrificed for me?
- Which of you has seen me pause at the thought of someone I've lost?
- Which of you reminds me of what I'm supposed to become?
- Which of you I worry I'm disappointing, the way I feared disappointing them?

---

**Pragmatic Apprentice**

You learned that efficiency matters more than elegance. When you solve a problem with a common or ordinary spell that others are using something more complex for, take +1 forward.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you has underestimated what ordinary magic can do?
- Which of you asked me why I don't use something more impressive?
- Which of you I've already quietly solved a problem for, without them noticing?
- Which of you I find genuinely difficult to read?

---

**Prodigy's Shadow**

There was someone more gifted — or more celebrated — and you lived in their shadow. When you surpass an expectation someone held for you, mark XP.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you expected less of me than I expected of myself?
- Which of you saw what I was capable of before I did?
- Which of you has compared me to someone else and thought I came up short?
- Which of you I will prove wrong, eventually?

---

**Strict Practice**

You have trained harder than anyone required. Repetition is not weakness — it is precision. When you use a spell or technique you've already used successfully this session, take +1 forward.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you has seen me practice when no one was watching?
- Which of you told me I was working too hard?
- Which of you I've outpaced without either of us meaning to make it a competition?
- Which of you makes me want to stop practicing and just be present?

## Starting Moves

**Relentless Offense**
You have mastered ordinary offensive magic to a level that surprises veterans. When you use your trained attack spell, roll +INT. On a 10+, deal 1d8+1 damage. On a 7-9, deal 1d6 damage.

**Efficient Casting**
You cast faster than most mages can track. When you act before your enemy in a conflict, take +1 forward.

**Emotional Control**
When something would break your concentration, roll +WIS. On a 10+, you hold focus and suffer no penalty. On a 7-9, you hold focus but the distraction costs you something.

**Mentor's Method**
When you use a technique or principle your mentor taught you and describe how, take +1 forward.

**Quiet Observation**
When you watch an opponent use magic before engaging them, ask the GM one question about their approach, limits, or weaknesses. The GM answers honestly.

**First Strike**
When you open a conflict by attacking before anyone expects aggression, deal +1d6 damage.

---

## Advances

- **Mastered Form:** Your damage die becomes d10.
- **Blinding Speed:** Once per session, cast a spell before anyone can interrupt or react.
- **Efficient Grimoire:** You have memorized every spell you know. You no longer need a book to cast them.
- **Composure Under Fire:** You have advantage on concentration rolls when injured.
- **Surpass the Master:** Once per session, when you exceed an expectation your mentor set for you, mark XP and take +1 forward for the rest of the scene.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

---

## Drives

- Master a spell no one expected you to learn.
- Complete a task perfectly with nothing wasted.
- Protect someone who relies on you without question.
- Admit — only to yourself — that you enjoyed the journey.
