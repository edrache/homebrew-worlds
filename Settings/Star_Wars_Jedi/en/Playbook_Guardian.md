## Background

Pick one.

---

**Peacekeeper**

You were trained specifically to protect the weak and uphold Republic law. When you **Defend** someone in your care, on a 10+ choose 3 instead of 2.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you has been in danger I could have prevented?
- Which of you has seen me hesitate when I should have acted?
- Which of you did I rescue once — and how do you feel about owing me that?
- Which of you fights dirty, and do I approve?

---

**Temple Blade**

You competed in the Temple's combat trials and placed among the top of your class. You start with the move **Form Mastery** even if you haven't taken it as an advance.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you has sparred with me and lost?
- Which of you thinks I rely too much on my blade?
- Which of you has seen the Form I use and understood what it says about me?
- Which of you has a fighting style that still surprises me?

---

**War Veteran**

You served in open conflict — the Clone Wars, border skirmish, pirate suppression — before your Knighting. When you **Hack and Slash** against someone who is actively trying to kill you, deal +1d6 damage.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you has never been in real combat before?
- Which of you saw me do something in battle that you've never told anyone?
- Which of you was hurt because of an order I gave?
- Which of you thinks I've seen too much?

---

**Bodyguard**

You were assigned to protect a dignitary before your Knighting and learned to put others first. When someone in your presence takes damage, you may choose to take that damage instead.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you am I still protecting out of habit?
- Which of you keeps doing things that make my job harder?
- Which of you knows who I was assigned to guard — and what happened to them?
- Which of you has saved my life in return?

## Starting Moves

**Saber Discipline**
When you **Hack and Slash** with your lightsaber, you may roll +WIS instead of +STR.

**Deflect**
When a ranged attack hits you, roll +DEX. On a 10+, you deflect it completely. On a 7–9, you deflect it but choose one: you are off-balance (disadvantage on your next roll), or you redirect it but not at a target of your choice.

**Force Push**
When you hurl someone or something away with the Force, roll +WIS. On a 10+, they are hurled back and off-balance. On a 7–9, choose one: they stumble back, or they are pulled off-balance but you both stay close.

**Guardian's Stance**
When you **Defend** with your lightsaber drawn, hold 1 extra on a 7+.

**Watchful Eye**
When you study a combat situation before acting, roll +WIS. On a 10+, ask the GM two questions about threats or weaknesses in the scene. On a 7–9, ask one.

**Knight's Resolve**
When you act despite fear, pain, or doubt, roll +CON. On a 10+, ignore the effect and act with full strength. On a 7–9, act, but the GM holds 1 against you to use later.

---

## Advances

- **Form Mastery:** When you **Hack and Slash**, on a 12+ you also disarm, knock back, or terrify your opponent (your choice).
- **Twin Blades:** You may wield two lightsabers. When you do, deal +1 damage on a 10+.
- **Shatterpoint:** Once per scene, ask the GM "what is the weakest point here?" The GM must answer honestly.
- **Unbreakable:** When you would be taken out, roll +CON. On a 10+, you stay in the fight with 1 HP.
- **Blade Mastery:** Your lightsaber damage die increases to d12.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

---

## Drives

- Protect someone weaker than yourself from harm.
- Defeat a worthy opponent in single combat.
- Refuse to strike down a defeated enemy.
- Stand between innocents and something terrible.
