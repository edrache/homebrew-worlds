## Background

Pick one.

---

**Exile's Path**

You left the Order voluntarily or were released by the Council to walk the living Force alone. Take +1 when you **Spout Lore** about the natural world, living creatures, or wild places. You also start with a loyal non-combat creature companion — ask your GM for details.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you knows why I left the Order?
- Which of you has followed a path as strange as mine?
- Which of you has asked me to come back — and what did I say?
- Which of you understands what I've given up?

---

**Living Force**

You were taught — by Qui-Gon Jinn or a lineage that traces to him — to trust the present moment over the grand design. When you act entirely on instinct without preparation, roll +WIS. On a 10+, the Force guides you perfectly. On a 7–9, you end up somewhere unexpected but unharmed.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you has seen me do something that shouldn't have worked?
- Which of you thinks I am reckless when I call it trust?
- Which of you followed my instinct once — and what happened?
- Which of you shares the same teacher in their lineage?

---

**Force Ghost's Student**

You have communed with a Jedi who has become one with the Force. Once per session, you may ask them one question. The answer is always true, but never complete.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you knows who guides me?
- Which of you has tried to listen and heard nothing?
- Which of you do the voices mention?
- Which of you has reason to fear what my teacher might know?

---

**The Long Road**

You have traveled more of the galaxy than most Jedi ever do. When you arrive somewhere new, roll +WIS. On a 10+, you've been here before or know someone who has — ask the GM for one useful piece of local knowledge. On a 7–9, you know something, but it is out of date.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you have I met before, in a place neither of us expected?
- Which of you is looking for something I might lead them to?
- Which of you has never left their home sector?
- Which of you carries something I recognize from a world I've visited?

## Starting Moves

**Living Force Awareness**
You are always attuned to the living things around you. In a natural or wild environment, the Force warns you of danger before it arrives. You are never surprised by creatures or natural hazards.

**Wanderer's Blade**
Your lightsaber is old and travel-worn, but perfectly maintained. When you **Hack and Slash**, on a 12+ choose one: deal double damage, your opponent's weapon is damaged or destroyed, or you end the fight without further bloodshed.

**Force Attunement**
When you commune with a location, creature, or object in the Force, roll +WIS. On a 10+, you learn its history, emotional resonance, and current state. On a 7–9, you learn one of the three.

**Traveler's Peace**
When you sit and eat with strangers, offer help freely, or show trust first, roll +CHA. On a 10+, they treat you as a trusted friend for the rest of the scene. On a 7–9, they are cautious but open.

**Unattached**
You carry no rank, no assignment, no political weight. When someone would use your institutional role against you — threatening your Order, pulling rank, leveraging your standing — it doesn't apply. You have no Order to threaten.

**The Will of the Force**
Once per session, describe what you believe the Force is pointing toward. The GM must confirm or deny it with a single word. If confirmed, the GM will arrange for you to find it.

## Advances

- **Midi-chlorian Speech:** You can communicate with any living creature through the Force, however primitive.
- **Force Healing:** When you lay hands on someone and focus the Force, roll +WIS. On a 10+, heal their damage die + your WIS. On a 7–9, heal their damage die.
- **Pathfinder:** When you lead a group through unknown territory, on a 10+ the GM cannot introduce hostile encounters. On a 7–9, choose one: safe passage or good time, not both.
- **Spirit Bond:** You may ask your Force Ghost two questions per session instead of one.
- **One with the Living Force:** When you die, roll +WIS. On a 10+, you become a Force Ghost and may remain with the group as long as you choose.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

## Drives

- Follow where the Force leads, even if you can't explain why.
- Leave a place better than you found it.
- Help someone who asks for nothing in return.
- Trust a stranger.
