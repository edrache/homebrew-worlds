## Background

Pick one.

---

**Diplomat**

You have represented the Jedi Order in formal negotiations. When you **Parley** using the weight of the Order behind you, on a 7+ you always have a real chance of success — the GM cannot simply say there is no leverage.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you has seen me talk someone down from violence?
- Which of you thinks I am too soft for the real galaxy?
- Which of you did I speak up for at the Temple when no one else would?
- Which of you carries a secret I helped them keep?

---

**Lore Keeper**

You studied the deeper archives of the Jedi Temple. When you **Spout Lore** about the Force, ancient history, or alien cultures, take +1.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you came to me for knowledge they couldn't find elsewhere?
- Which of you distrusts what I know because of where I learned it?
- Which of you carries something whose origins I recognized immediately?
- Which of you has asked a question I still haven't answered?

---

**Force Sensitive**

Your connection to the Force is unusually strong and unfiltered. Once per session, you may declare a Force vision as true — the GM must work it into the fiction.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you has appeared in my visions?
- Which of you finds my sensitivity unsettling?
- Which of you trusts my instincts even when they can't explain why?
- Which of you have I warned about something that came true?

---

**Mediator**

You have settled disputes between factions, species, and cultures. When you step in to stop a conflict between two parties, both sides pause — the GM cannot immediately escalate unless a PC does something provocative.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you have I talked out of a fight they would have won?
- Which of you resents me for stopping something they wanted to finish?
- Which of you came to me when they couldn't resolve something on their own?
- Which of you do I still need to reconcile with someone?

## Starting Moves

**Mind Trick**
When you attempt to influence a weak-willed being with the Force, roll +CHA. On a 10+, they comply with a simple suggestion. On a 7–9, they comply but something costs you — they remember more than they should, or someone notices.

**Force Sense**
When you open yourself to the Force to read a situation or person, roll +WIS. On a 10+, ask the GM three questions. On a 7–9, ask one.
- What does this person truly want?
- Is there danger here, and from where?
- What has recently happened in this place?
- Who here is hiding something?

**Telekinesis**
When you move objects with the Force, roll +WIS. On a 10+, you control it precisely. On a 7–9, you move it but the GM introduces a complication — too fast, too far, or too noticed.

**Calming Presence**
When you spend a moment in focused presence with someone who is frightened or angry, they lose that condition. They may gain another if the situation doesn't change.

**Force Bond**
Choose one other PC. You share a Force bond. When either of you is in mortal danger, the other always knows, regardless of distance. When you act to help each other directly, both of you take +1.

**Unarmored Serenity**
You do not wear armor. When you take damage, roll +WIS. On a 10+, reduce the damage by your WIS modifier. On a 7–9, reduce it by 1.

## Advances

- **Deep Rapport:** Your Force Bond can include a second PC.
- **Farsight:** Once per session, ask the GM a yes/no question about the future. They must answer honestly, but vaguely.
- **Force Stasis:** When you concentrate fully, you can hold a creature or object completely still for as long as you focus. You can do nothing else while maintaining it.
- **Words of Power:** Your Mind Trick works on strong-willed beings, but on a 7–9 the GM holds 2 against you instead of 1.
- **Master Negotiator:** When you **Parley**, a 7–9 result becomes a 10+ result. On a 10+, you get what you want and they owe you a favor.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

## Drives

- Resolve a conflict without violence.
- Share knowledge that prevents suffering.
- Protect someone's dignity when others would strip it.
- Act on a Force vision despite uncertainty.
