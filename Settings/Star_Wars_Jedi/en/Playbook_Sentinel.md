## Background

Pick one.

---

**Shadow**

You were trained by the Jedi Shadows to hunt dark side artifacts and Force cults. You always know when you are being followed. You start with a holdout blaster (close/near, loud, small).

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you knows what I do in the Order's name?
- Which of you has asked me something I refused to answer?
- Which of you have I watched without you knowing?
- Which of you found out something about me I didn't intend to share?

---

**Investigator**

You worked alongside the Republic Security Bureau or a planetary law enforcement body. When you **Discern Realities** at a crime scene or during an interrogation, ask one additional question beyond what the roll grants.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you helped me close a case I couldn't crack alone?
- Which of you was once a suspect in something I was investigating?
- Which of you has questions about how a case we shared ended?
- Which of you knows I bent the rules to get a result?

---

**Infiltrator**

You've gone undercover in criminal organizations, dark-side cults, or enemy territory. You can create a convincing false identity for yourself with a few hours of preparation. On a 7+ when maintaining a cover, it holds.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you knew me under a different name?
- Which of you knows a cover story about me that isn't true?
- Which of you has covered for me when my identity was almost blown?
- Which of you isn't sure which version of me is real?

---

**Tracker**

You were trained in pursuit — by land, by space, through the Force. When you track someone across difficult terrain or through a city, you always find the trail. The GM can make it dangerous or costly, but never impossible.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you have I been sent to find before?
- Which of you knows someone I am still looking for?
- Which of you ran from something — and have I asked you about it?
- Which of you helped me catch someone, and how do you feel about what happened to them?

## Starting Moves

**Read the Room**
When you enter a new location and take a moment to observe, roll +WIS. On a 10+, ask three questions. On a 7–9, ask one.
- Who here is armed and dangerous?
- Who is in charge, and who resents them?
- What is being hidden or lied about?
- Where is the quickest exit?

**Shadow Step**
When you move silently and unseen, roll +DEX. On a 10+, you are not detected and in position. On a 7–9, you are in position but choose one: you make a small noise, you leave a trace, or you are briefly glimpsed.

**Force Empathy**
When you touch an object or place saturated with strong emotion, you experience a flash of what happened there. The GM tells you one true thing about a significant recent event.

**Dual Strike**
When you **Hack and Slash** with a weapon in each hand, roll twice and take the higher result.

**Underworld Contacts**
You maintain a network of informants in the grey economy. When you seek information in an urban area, roll +CHA. On a 10+, you find it quickly. On a 7–9, the information is available but comes with a complication or cost.

**Force Cloak**
When you concentrate and become still in the Force, you fade from Force-sensitive perception. Darksiders and Force adepts cannot sense your presence while you maintain this focus.

## Advances

- **Perfect Disguise:** Your false identities are flawless. Even Force users cannot sense the deception unless you reveal yourself.
- **Ambush:** When you **Hack and Slash** from hiding, deal +2d6 damage.
- **Force Interrogation:** When you press someone for truth through the Force, roll +WIS. On a 10+, they answer one question honestly. On a 7–9, they answer but know you've touched their mind.
- **Ghost:** When you move through an area being actively searched for you, roll +DEX. On a 10+, you pass unseen. On a 7–9, choose one: you pass, or you leave no trace.
- **Blade Dancer:** When you **Hack and Slash**, on a 7–9 you do not take the enemy's counter-attack.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

## Drives

- Uncover a deception that others have accepted as truth.
- Protect someone without letting them know you were there.
- Follow a lead to its end, no matter where it goes.
- Choose mercy when justice would have been easier.
