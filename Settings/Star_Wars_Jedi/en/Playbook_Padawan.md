*You are not yet a Knight. But the Force chose you, and that means something.*

## Background

Pick one.

---

**Chosen**

You were identified as Force-sensitive unusually late, or your midi-chlorian count is exceptionally high. Once per session you may treat a roll as a 12+ by accepting a dark-side temptation — the GM offers, and you choose whether to take it.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you was there when I first demonstrated unusual power?
- Which of you worries about what I might become?
- Which of you believes in me more than the Council does?
- Which of you has seen me lose control of my power?

---

**Survivor**

You were found on a war-torn world or escaped slavery before coming to the Temple. When you endure hardship, deprivation, or pain, take +1 ongoing until the situation changes.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you knows where I came from?
- Which of you has asked me about my life before the Temple — and what did I tell you?
- Which of you protected me when I was still too angry to protect myself?
- Which of you sees the galaxy the same hard way I do?

---

**The Master's Shadow**

Your Master is well-known — respected, feared, or controversial. When you invoke your Master's reputation, roll +CHA. On a 10+, it opens doors and creates respect. On a 7–9, it creates complications as well.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you knows my Master personally?
- Which of you thinks I'll never step out of my Master's shadow?
- Which of you has offered to teach me something my Master hasn't?
- Which of you keeps a secret that involves my Master?

---

**Prodigy**

You excel beyond your years in one discipline. Choose: combat, Force powers, or lore. When you act within that discipline, treat a 7–9 as a 10+. But your failures draw more scrutiny — the Council is watching you.

When you introduce yourself to the other PCs, ask one or more of the following:
- Which of you tested my abilities and was surprised?
- Which of you thinks my talent is a burden as much as a gift?
- Which of you has helped me when I failed despite my skill?
- Which of you thinks I'm not as talented as the Council believes?

## Starting Moves

**Still Learning**
When you **Spout Lore** or attempt a move outside your training, on a miss you still learn something true. The GM tells you one honest fact about the situation even as things go wrong.

**Apprentice's Blade**
When you **Hack and Slash** with your lightsaber, you may roll +WIS instead of +STR. On a 12+, your Master's training guides your hand — deal maximum damage.

**Raw Force**
When you push yourself beyond your training and channel raw Force energy, roll +WIS. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7–9, choose 1 but take –1 forward.
- Deal d10 damage to a nearby target.
- Knock everyone near you off their feet.
- Move a heavy object across the room.
- Shield yourself from an incoming attack.

**Bond with Master**
Name your Master (a GM character). When your Master is present, take +1 to all rolls. When your Master is endangered, roll +WIS to act rationally rather than impulsively.

**Quick Study**
When another PC teaches you something through demonstration, you can use their move once before the end of the session.

**Earnest Heart**
When you appeal to someone's better nature with genuine honesty — no tricks, no leverage — roll +CHA. On a 10+, they respond honestly in kind. On a 7–9, they soften but ask something of you.

## Advances

- **Knighting:** You are raised to Knight. Rename this playbook and choose a class move from any other Jedi playbook. You lose Bond with Master.
- **Force Intuition:** You always know when something is wrong, even when you can't explain it.
- **Growing Power:** Your Raw Force now gives 3 choices on a 10+ and 2 on a 7–9.
- **Reckless Strength:** Once per session, you may succeed automatically on a physical Force use. The GM names a consequence you must accept.
- **The Force Protects:** When you would die, roll +WIS. On a 10+, you survive with 1 HP and a vision from the Force.
- **Improved Stat:** Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +2.
- **Superior Stat** *(Requires: Improved Stat, at least one other advance)*: Increase one of your stats by +1, to a max of +3.

---

## Drives

- Make your Master proud in a moment that truly matters.
- Admit you were wrong and make it right.
- Stand up for someone when your Master would have stayed silent.
- Resist the temptation of a shortcut.
